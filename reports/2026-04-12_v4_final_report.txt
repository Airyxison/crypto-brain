============================================================
ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
============================================================

1. ETH STRUCTURAL FAILURE: ETH converged to no-trade policy in all 3
   iterations where no-trade was available as an option. The 1% stop
   paired with ETH's intraday volatility makes it near-impossible to
   profit; the agent learned this and refused to enter.

2. STOP-LOSS MISMATCH (BTC, SOL): The 1% DEFAULT_STOP_PCT is too tight
   for BTC and SOL volatility regimes. Active policies achieved 23-29%
   win rates with 70-90% of exits via stop-loss, leading to negative
   expected value per trade.

3. ADA SUCCESS PATTERN: ADA's range-bound, lower-volatility behavior
   allows the 1% stop to function as intended. The v4 policy emerged as
   a high-conviction, low-frequency strategy (7 trades / 100k ticks)
   with long holds (128 bars avg) and ADJ_STOP trailing to lock profits.
   This is the correct learned behavior for this reward structure.

4. MIN_HOLD_BARS SENSITIVITY: Doubling from 20→40 in v3 caused policy
   collapse in 3/4 assets. The reward signal became too sparse/negative
   for agents to learn anything useful within 200k steps.
